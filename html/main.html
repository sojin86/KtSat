Service

Data & Telecoms

Where ground network is difficult to implement,
satellites are the only viable option for connectivity.
KT SAT provides managed network services including data,
voice, video conference and other solutions to rural
and overseas isolated areas, by using dedicated
communication network from the KOREASAT fleet.

Broadcast & Media

Reach your customers through transmitting, receiving
and distributing video contents via the KOREASAT
fleet, Kumsan Satellite Service Center, and our
international fiber optic network. KT SAT provides
24-hour customer support for stable domestic and
overseas contents services.

Mobility

KT sat provides the best mobility services such as
MVSAT, Inmarsat, Maritime Solution and IFC(In-Flight
Connectivity)

Government

Benefit from KT SAT’s extensive experience in
customized satellite solutions and system integration
for critical government & military missions. Our skillful
technicians and satellite facilities can fully support the
operation of various satellite services as well as
demonstration and testing on the ground, sea, and air.

Teleport

Hosting and managing service by utilizing kt sat's
facilities for customers with difficulties in satellite
communication.
